Barcelona reaching agreement with Bayern over Lewandoski.

August 30, 2022 12:30

Lewandowski is Barcelona's top priority

Barcelona have a handful of transfer targets but none sit higher on their list of priorities than Bayern Munich striker Robert Lewandowski.

Although the club have recently seemed to play down the importance of the operation, the truth is that it is very much still on their mind
Talks with the player are at an advanced stage but there is still a long way to go in reaching agreement with Bayern over a fee.

The Bavarians are strong financially and are not a club that tend to sell their stars on the cheap, nor without a fight.

Although there was initially talk that the deal could be worth around 30 million euros, the latest estimates indicate that it will be closer to 50m euros. - according to Marca.
What plays massively in Barcelona's favour is Lewandowski's overwhelming desire to join the club.

The striker has publicly voiced his determination to leave Bayern and has his heart set on a move to the Camp Nou.
