Casemiro's response to not playing in UCL with Man Utd this season

August 30, 2022 01:30

Casemiro delivered a superb reaction and response when asked about not playing in the Champions League

having won the competition five times with Real Madrid, Casemiro has no issues about not playing in the Champions League this season and delivered an expert response following his move to Manchester United from European Champion Real Madrid.

The Red Devils completed the signing of the 30-year-old this week for a fee in the region of £60million plus, making the second most expensive player over the age of 30 behind Cristiano Ronaldo. Casemiro was unveiled on the pitch at Old Trafford on the evening of United's 2-1 win over Liverpool and received a warm reception from the home faithful as Ten Hag completed his fourth signing of the summer window.

Casemiro leaves Real after winning the Champions League and La Liga double last season. He played an influential role in beating Liverpool in the final in Paris and departs the Santiago Bernabau as a club legend

Meanwhile, United finished with their lowest-ever Premier League points tally last season and will compete in the Europa League this season. Joining the Red Devils is a step down for the Real Madrid icon, but playing in Europe's second tier is not something that bothers Casemiro.

When asked about missing out on playing Champions League football, he told ESPN Brasil : "Well, I already have five titles"

The smart response caught the attention of United supporters who took to social media in their hundreds to acknowledge it, suggesting Casemiro is already a fan favourite despite the fact he is yet to pull on a Unites shirt in anger.
